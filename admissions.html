Admissions — ScottsTechX

Begin your journey into cybersecurity and full-stack development. Review requirements, the application process, and fee structure below.

📋 Admission Requirements

Academic Prerequisites

  • Minimum 5 O-Level passes including English and Mathematics
  • A-Level or equivalent in relevant field preferred
  • Basic computer literacy (Windows/Linux operations)
  • Understanding of fundamental networking concepts

Technical Background

  • No prior programming experience required
  • Familiarity with command-line interfaces is a plus
  • Own laptop/desktop with minimum 8GB RAM
  • Stable internet connection for remote components

Language Proficiency

  • English reading and writing proficiency
  • Ability to follow technical documentation in English
  • Communication skills for team collaboration

Commitment & Conduct

  • Dedication of 15–20 hours per week to coursework
  • Agreement to ethical use of knowledge
  • Respect for peer learning environment
  • Attendance at live sessions when scheduled

🔁 Application Process

1

Submit Application Form

Fill out the application form below with accurate personal, academic, and contact details. Incomplete applications will not be reviewed.

2

Document Verification

Provide copies of your academic certificates, national ID or passport, and proof of address. Our admissions team will verify all documents within 3–5 business days.

3

Entrance Assessment

Applicants may be asked to complete a short aptitude assessment covering basic logic, problem-solving, and motivation. This is to ensure the program is a good fit for you.

4

Interview (if required)

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a virtual interview with the program coordinator. This is a casual conversation to discuss your goals and expectations.

5

Offer & Enrollment

Successful applicants receive an offer letter via email. Upon acceptance, you will receive login credentials for the student portal and your first cohort assignment.
